Important Disclaimer 重要声明

This screening tool is NOT a diagnostic instrument. It is based on developmental milestones from the M-CHAT-R/F and other validated screening tools, adapted for educational purposes. Only a qualified healthcare professional can diagnose autism spectrum disorder. If you have concerns about your child's development, please consult your pediatrician.

此筛查工具不是诊断工具。它基于M-CHAT-R/F及其他经过验证的筛查工具中的发育里程碑，仅用于教育目的。只有合格的医疗专业人员才能诊断自闭症谱系障碍。如果您对孩子的发育有任何担忧，请咨询您的儿科医生。
