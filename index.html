Our Mission 我们的使命

We bridge the gap between Chinese American families and autism support services by providing information in both English and Chinese, addressing cultural concerns, and connecting families with the resources they need.

我们通过提供中英双语信息、关注文化差异并将家庭与所需资源联系起来，架起华裔美国家庭与自闭症支持服务之间的桥梁。

Why This Matters 为什么这很重要

1 in 36

Children in the U.S. are diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder (CDC, 2023).

美国儿童被诊断为自闭症谱系障碍（CDC, 2023年）。

Underdiagnosed

Asian American children are often diagnosed later than their peers due to cultural stigma and language barriers.

由于文化偏见和语言障碍，亚裔美国儿童通常比同龄人更晚被诊断。

Language Gap

Many Chinese American families struggle to access autism information and services in their preferred language.

许多华裔美国家庭难以用他们偏好的语言获取自闭症信息和服务。
