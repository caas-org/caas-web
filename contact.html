⚠️ Need Immediate Help? ⚠️ 需要立即帮助？

If you or your child are in crisis, please contact:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (available in multiple languages)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency: Call 911
