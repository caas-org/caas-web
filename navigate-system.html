Insurance Coverage for Autism Services 自闭症服务的保险覆盖

Understanding your insurance coverage is one of the most important steps in accessing autism services. Here's what you need to know:

Federal and State Mandates

Under the Affordable Care Act (ACA) and most state laws, health insurance plans are required to cover autism-related services. Coverage varies by state but generally includes:

  • Diagnostic evaluations — The initial assessment to determine if your child has ASD
  • Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) — Most states mandate ABA coverage, though hours may be limited
  • Speech therapy — Often covered as a medically necessary service
  • Occupational therapy — Covered when prescribed by a physician
  • Mental health services — Counseling and psychiatric services

Steps to Maximize Your Coverage

  1. Review your plan: Call your insurance company and ask specifically about autism/ASD coverage. Get the details in writing.
  2. Get pre-authorization: Many services require pre-approval. Ask your provider's office to handle this.
  3. Keep detailed records: Save all referrals, prescriptions, evaluation reports, and communication with insurance.
  4. Appeal denials: If a service is denied, you have the right to appeal. Many denials are overturned on appeal.
  5. Ask about language services: Insurance companies are required to provide interpreters and translated documents upon request.

了解您的保险覆盖范围是获取自闭症服务最重要的步骤之一。以下是您需要知道的：

联邦和州法规

根据《平价医疗法案》（ACA）和大多数州法律，健康保险计划必须覆盖与自闭症相关的服务。覆盖范围因州而异，但通常包括：

  • 诊断评估 — 确定您的孩子是否患有ASD的初始评估
  • 应用行为分析（ABA） — 大多数州要求覆盖ABA，但小时数可能有限
  • 言语治疗 — 通常作为医学必要服务被覆盖
  • 职业治疗 — 由医生开具处方时被覆盖
  • 心理健康服务 — 咨询和精神科服务

最大化您的覆盖范围的步骤

  1. 审查您的计划：致电您的保险公司，具体询问自闭症/ASD的覆盖范围。获取书面详细信息。
  2. 获得预先授权：许多服务需要预先批准。请您的服务提供者的办公室处理此事。
  3. 保留详细记录：保存所有转介、处方、评估报告和与保险公司的通信。
  4. 对拒绝提出申诉：如果服务被拒绝，您有权提出申诉。许多拒绝在申诉后被推翻。
  5. 询问语言服务：保险公司在接到请求后必须提供口译员和翻译文件。

Medicaid: If your family qualifies, Medicaid covers comprehensive autism services with little to no out-of-pocket costs. Many states also offer Medicaid waivers specifically for children with developmental disabilities, regardless of family income. Contact your state's Medicaid office for details.

Medicaid：如果您的家庭符合资格，Medicaid以很少或无自付费用覆盖全面的自闭症服务。许多州还专门为发育障碍儿童提供Medicaid豁免，不论家庭收入如何。请联系您所在州的Medicaid办公室了解详情。

Education Rights: IEP and 504 Plans 教育权利：IEP和504计划

Your child has legal rights to receive a free, appropriate public education (FAPE) under federal law. Here are the two main programs you should know about:

IEP (Individualized Education Program)

An IEP is a legally binding document that outlines the special education services your child will receive. It is provided under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA).

  • Requires a formal evaluation by the school
  • Includes specific, measurable goals
  • Provides specialized instruction and related services
  • Reviewed and updated annually
  • Re-evaluated every 3 years

504 Plan

A 504 Plan provides accommodations and modifications in the regular classroom. It is provided under Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act.

  • Less formal than an IEP
  • Provides accommodations (e.g., extra time on tests, seating preference)
  • Does not include specialized instruction
  • Available to students who do not qualify for an IEP
  • Protects against disability discrimination

How to Request an Evaluation

  1. Write a formal letter: Send a written request to your school's principal or special education coordinator asking for an evaluation. Keep a copy for your records.
  2. The school has 60 days: After receiving your written consent, the school must complete the evaluation within 60 calendar days (timeline varies by state).
  3. Attend the IEP meeting: You have the right to participate in all meetings. You can bring a friend, advocate, or interpreter.
  4. Review and sign: Review the proposed IEP carefully. You do not have to sign immediately — you can take it home to review.
  5. Know your rights: You can disagree with the school's evaluation or IEP. You have the right to request an Independent Educational Evaluation (IEE) at the school's expense.

根据联邦法律，您的孩子有权接受免费、适当的公共教育（FAPE）。以下是您应该了解的两个主要项目：

IEP（个别化教育计划）

IEP是一份具有法律约束力的文件，概述了您的孩子将接受的特殊教育服务。它是根据《残疾人教育法》（IDEA）提供的。

  • 需要学校的正式评估
  • 包括具体的、可衡量的目标
  • 提供专门的教学和相关服务
  • 每年审查和更新
  • 每3年重新评估

504计划

504计划在普通教室中提供适应和修改。它是根据《康复法案》第504条提供的。

  • 比IEP更不正式
  • 提供适应措施（如考试额外时间、座位偏好）
  • 不包括专门的教学
  • 适用于不符合IEP资格的学生
  • 防止残疾歧视

如何请求评估

  1. 撰写正式信函：向学校校长或特殊教育协调员发送书面请求，要求进行评估。保留一份副本。
  2. 学校有60天：在收到您的书面同意后，学校必须在60个日历天内完成评估（各州时间可能不同）。
  3. 参加IEP会议：您有权参加所有会议。您可以带一位朋友、倡导者或翻译。
  4. 审查并签署：仔细审查提议的IEP。您不必立即签署——您可以带回家审查。
  5. 了解您的权利：您可以不同意学校的评估或IEP。您有权要求学校出资进行独立教育评估（IEE）。

Important: You have the right to an interpreter at all school meetings. Federal law (Title VI of the Civil Rights Act) requires schools to communicate with parents in a language they understand. If the school does not provide an interpreter, request one in writing.

重要提示：您有权在所有学校会议上获得口译服务。联邦法律（《民权法案》第六章）要求学校用家长能理解的语言与家长沟通。如果学校没有提供口译员，请以书面形式提出要求。

Early Intervention Services (Ages 0-3) 早期干预服务（0-3岁）

Under Part C of IDEA, every state must offer Early Intervention (EI) services for infants and toddlers with developmental delays. These services are a critical first step.

What to Expect

  • Free evaluation: Contact your state's Early Intervention program for a free developmental evaluation
  • IFSP: If eligible, your child will receive an Individualized Family Service Plan (IFSP) within 45 days
  • Services at home: Most EI services are provided in your home or community setting
  • Types of services: Speech therapy, occupational therapy, developmental therapy, family training
  • Low or no cost: Most EI services are free or on a sliding scale based on family income
  • Transition planning: At age 3, the EI team helps transition your child to school-based services

根据IDEA的C部分，每个州必须为有发育迟缓的婴幼儿提供早期干预（EI）服务。这些服务是关键的第一步。

期望什么

  • 免费评估：联系您所在州的早期干预项目进行免费发育评估
  • IFSP：如果符合资格，您的孩子将在45天内获得个别化家庭服务计划（IFSP）
  • 家庭服务：大多数EI服务在您的家中或社区环境中提供
  • 服务类型：言语治疗、职业治疗、发育治疗、家庭培训
  • 低成本或免费：大多数EI服务是免费的或根据家庭收入按比例收费
  • 过渡计划：在3岁时，EI团队帮助将您的孩子过渡到学校服务

How to start: Search for "[Your State] Early Intervention Program" or call your pediatrician to request a referral. You do not need a doctor's referral — any parent can request an evaluation.

如何开始：搜索"[您的州] Early Intervention Program"或致电您的儿科医生请求转介。您不需要医生的转介——任何家长都可以要求评估。
